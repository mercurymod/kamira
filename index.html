What is Kamira?

Kamira is an open source software project designed to measure Clinical Quality Measures (CQMs) based on the availability of their supporting data, their financial impact, and the complexity of their calculations.

Objective

Kamira will provide an automated and unbiased method for evaluating Clinical Quality Measures (CQMs). Kamira will identify the particular metrics that are the most valuable for assessing the quality of Clinical Quality Measures.
